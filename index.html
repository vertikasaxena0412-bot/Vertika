⚠ The Problem (Avalara)

"Fortune 500 companies need to manage HTS classification, FTA eligibility, duty drawback, and denied party screening across hundreds of markets — in real time, at millions of transactions per month. No existing tool handles all of it natively. Manual processes create risk and delay."

🔍 My Diagnosis

The root issue was that global trade compliance was treated as a legal function, not an operational one. The logic existed in people's heads and in spreadsheets. To scale it, you had to systematize the logic — build rules engines that could make the same decisions a compliance expert would, consistently, at volume.

⚡ What I Built (with engineers & architects)
  • HS classification engine — automated tariff code determination across 180+ markets
  • FTA eligibility module — rules of origin logic for USMCA, CETA, CPTPP and more
  • ECCN/ABI determination — export control classification for dual-use goods
  • Duty drawback module — automated recovery of overpaid duties
  • Denied party screening — real-time sanctions and watchlist checks
📈 The Outcome

The platform processed 10M+ transactions per month for Fortune 500 clients. Audit exposure reduced by 40%. Zonos hired me to replicate the same infrastructure for their business. Thomson Reuters brought me in to deploy their equivalent system at global scale. The work compounded.

Build once. Sell to Fortune 500. Get hired to build it again. That's proof of concept.
